Who We Are

More Than a Service Provider

Axiom Business Tech was founded on a powerful belief: that growing businesses deserve access to the same quality of financial systems and data intelligence that large corporations use.

We combine a strong ACCA-based accounting foundation with modern technology tools — Power BI, Python, SQL, and automation frameworks — to deliver solutions that are both financially sound and technically powerful.

Whether you're a startup needing clean bookkeeping, an SME looking for business intelligence dashboards, or a growing company wanting to automate financial workflows — Axiom has the expertise to deliver.

Our clients span Pakistan, the UK, the USA, and beyond. We work with property managers, service-based companies, founders, and CEOs who are serious about running data-driven, financially clear operations.

Meet the Founder

The Mind Behind Axiom

Awais Tahir is a finance-focused entrepreneur with a strong foundation in accounting, business strategy, and data analytics. As the founder of Axiom Business Tech, he is building a modern solutions firm that integrates financial expertise with technology to help businesses streamline operations, automate processes, and make data-driven decisions.

With a forward-thinking mindset, Awais is actively working on bridging the gap between traditional finance and digital transformation — leveraging tools like Python, Power BI, SQL, and automation to create scalable business solutions that deliver real results.

Currently pursuing his ACCA qualification while simultaneously running Axiom Business Tech, Awais brings a rare combination of academic rigor and hands-on entrepreneurial experience to every client engagement.
