The Challenge
A UK-based property manager was manually compiling financial data from multiple spreadsheets each month — a process that took over 3 days and was prone to errors. They had no real-time view of NOI, rent collection, or expenses across their 40+ unit portfolio, making it impossible to make fast, confident decisions.
Our Solution
We designed and built a fully automated Power BI dashboard connected directly to their QuickBooks and Excel data sources. The dashboard updates automatically and gives the client a live view of every key metric in their portfolio.
What We Delivered
Live NOI tracking across all 40+ units with drill-down by property
Rent roll dashboard showing paid, outstanding and overdue tenants
Expense breakdown by property, category and month
Cash flow trend charts with month-over-month comparison
Occupancy rate and vacancy loss calculations
Automated data refresh — no manual updates ever needed

The Challenge
A tech startup came to Axiom with 2 years of completely disorganised financial records — transactions miscategorized, duplicate entries, missing invoices, and no coherent chart of accounts. They were approaching a funding round and needed clean, investor-ready books immediately.
Our Solution
We performed a full financial audit and cleanup — reviewing every transaction, correcting categorizations, rebuilding the chart of accounts from scratch, and constructing a SQL-backed reporting model that now powers their monthly financial reports automatically.
What We Delivered
Complete historical reconciliation across 2 years of transactions
Rebuilt chart of accounts optimized for their SaaS business model
Corrected P&L, balance sheet and cash flow for all periods
SQL database schema for financial data storage and querying
Automated monthly reporting model connected to live data
Investor-ready financial package for the funding round
